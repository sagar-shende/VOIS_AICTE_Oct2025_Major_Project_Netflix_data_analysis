## 📌 Problem Statement

Netflix hosts **8,000+ titles** across genres, countries, and categories. Without structured analysis, it's impossible to identify what drives engagement, which regions produce the most content, and how trends have evolved over time.

**Key Questions:**
- Which content type — Movies or TV Shows — dominates Netflix?
- Which countries produce the most content?
- How has Netflix content grown year-over-year?
- What are the most popular genres and ratings?

---

## 📊 Key Insights

| Insight | Finding |
|---|---|
| 🎬 Content Split | Movies **69.6%** vs TV Shows **30.4%** |
| 🌍 Top Country | USA leads with **3,211 titles** |
| 🇮🇳 India Rank | **#2 globally** with 972 titles (Bollywood presence) |
| 📈 Peak Growth | **2019** — 2,153 titles added (15x growth from 2015) |
| 🎭 Top Genres | Drama & Comedy dominate both Movies and TV Shows |
| 🔞 Top Rating | **TV-MA** — mature content is most common |
